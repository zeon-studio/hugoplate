## Introduction

Richard Bowles has worked in the Entertainments/Media Industry since the early 1990's.

He has specialised in Lighting for Multi-Camera Live Broadcasting since for over quarter of a century.

As part of this, for 15 years he worked for SKy Televtions (UK) as a Lighting Director, where he was responsible for the live studio lighting for Sky News, Sky Sports, and other channels.

He Designed, Built, and Supported multiple studios for both Sky News and Sky Sports.

He was the Series Lighitng Designer & Director for numerous long-running flagship productions for Sky Sports - Premier League - EFL - Rugby - Racing - F1; Sky Sports News; Sky News; Sky Arts; Sky Entertainments; Sky Poker; and more.
