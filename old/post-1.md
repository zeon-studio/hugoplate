# Why do you need Camera Lighting?

* Cameras are everywhere nowadays - with the majority of “Gen-Z”  considering themselves as "Content Creators"  - and every experience from birth to death is live-streamed across the world.


* People look at the concert in front of them through their mobile phone and share the key moments with their friends within seconds. The rest of the time they are looking at the big video screen.


* This makes the importance of your Event, your Product, your Experience, looking good on Camera being more critical than to the Eye.


* Fortunatly, if it looks good on camera, it also looks good to the human eye


* However, the reverse does not hold true.


* Cameras look at things from multiple angles at the same time - and in general from the side, rather than just front-on.


* They can jump instantly from a distant wide shot to a close-up on a face (try doing that with some binoculars!).


* Unlike human brains, cameras do not easily get distracted or diverted.


* How they focus on a subject and on other things is very different.


* Their reaction to dynamic changes in brightness levels is very different.
